About us

Kenyatta University Equity Leaders Chapter (KU~ELC)

Young Empowered Leaders

Kenyatta University Equity Leaders Chapter (KU~ELC) is a congeries of all Wings to Fly(WtF) alumni and Equity Leaders Programme(ELP) scholars at Kenyatta University. KU~ELC is not just a chapter or club but also a family for this assemblage of future leaders. As a team, we mainly stress on the following key aspects: unity amongst ourselves, equality in opportunities, networking, mentorship, leadership career development among other areas.
